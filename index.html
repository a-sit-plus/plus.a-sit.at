A-SIT Plus

A-SIT Plus GmbH specializes in providing advanced information security know-how to endeavors of both public- and private-sector organizations. We not only offer conceptual consulting, risk analysis, and planning/architecture, but also extend seamlessly into practical software development of critical security components, delivering innovative solutions that bridge research and practical application. Our activities focus on eGovernment solutions, identity management and authentication, mobile security, and security tooling.

Our Mission: Excellence in Digital Security

Established in July 2015, A-SIT Plus GmbH was created to meet the growing demand for comprehensive information security services. By offering tailored solutions to Austria's eGovernment initiatives, ministries, and private sector entities, we ensure a seamless integration of advanced security mechanisms into production environments.

Partnerships and Cooperation

Our success is rooted in strong collaborations. Through these alliances, we continue to provide cutting-edge security technologies that align with the evolving opportunities and demands of a connected world.

A non-profit research organization that advises the public sector on security innovations and assesses conformity with established standards.

ISEC

We are in close partnership with the Institute of Information Security (ISEC) at Graz University of Technology to foster innovation in security solutions.

Our Origin

As a wholly owned subsidiary of the A-SIT association — a non-profit organization focused on research and advisory services — we operate as a separate entity to provide specialized, project-specific assistance. This structure allows balancing the formal advisory duties for the Austrian public sector of the A-SIT association with actionable support in concrete projects for organizations looking to bolster their security frameworks via the A-SIT Plus GmbH.
