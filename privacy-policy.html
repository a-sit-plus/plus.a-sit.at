Privacy Policy

Overview

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not actively collect personal data. However, GitHub may collect personal data from visitors, including logs of visitor IP addresses, to comply with legal obligations, and to maintain the security and integrity of this website and the service. See the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

1. Purpose of the Privacy Policy

The A-SIT Plus GmbH stands by its commitment to protect your personal data. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Collection of Data

Since this website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not actively collect personal data. However, the following data may be passively collected by our hosting service or third-party tools:

  • Log Data: Information like your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamp of your visit may be logged by GitHub Pages.
  • Cookies: This site does not use cookies or tracking technologies unless explicitly stated.

Please refer to GitHub’s Privacy Policy for details on their handling of log data.

3. Use of Data

We do not process personal data collected through this site for any specific purposes. Any data collected by GitHub (e.g., server logs) is used to ensure the operational security and functionality of the hosting platform.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not share, sell, or lease your personal data. However, GitHub, as the hosting provider, may process your data according to their privacy policy.

5. Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you (GDPR, Art. 15)
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data (GDPR, Art. 16)
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions (GDPR, Art. 17)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit the way your data is processed under specific circumstances (GDPR, Art. 18)
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes (GDPR, Art. 18)
  • Right to Data Portability: Request that we transfer your data to another organization or directly to you (GDPR, Art. 20)

If the processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time; the processing is lawful based on the consent until the withdrawal.

If a data subject wishes to exercise the aforementioned data subject rights (pursuant to Art. 15, 16 or 17 GDPR), he or she may do so by contacting us at the contact details provided in Section 10.

If you are of the opinion that your data subject rights are not or not sufficiently complied with, you have the possibility to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (GDPR, Art. 77) with the competent data protection authority.

6. Data Retention

As a static website, we do not retain or store any personal data. Log data collected by GitHub is managed according to their retention policies.

7. Third-Party Services

This site may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

8. Security Measures

While this static website does not actively collect or store personal data, GitHub Pages ensures operational security. Please consult GitHub’s Security Overview for more details.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
